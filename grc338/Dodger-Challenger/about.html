Demon SRT

To handle all that power, the Demon Set Challenger features a number of upgrades, including an upgraded suspension system, larger brakes, and a wide body kit that allows for wider tires. It also has a number of unique design features, including Demon badging, a unique grille, and special graphics.

Overall, the Demon Set Challenger is a highly sought-after car among performance enthusiasts and collectors due to its impressive performance and unique design features. However, due to its limited production run, it can be difficult to find and often sells for a premium price.
